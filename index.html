Welcome to Warner Bros

BIBLIOGRAPHY

Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc., commonly known as Warner Bros., is one of the world's leading entertainment companies. Founded on April 4, 1923, by brothers Harry, Albert, Sam, and Jack Warner, the company is headquartered in Burbank, California. Warner Bros. is a major player in the global film and television industry, known for producing and distributing a wide range of content including movies, TV shows, and digital media.

ABOUT US

The company has an extensive film library, with many iconic franchises and classic films under its belt, such as the Harry Potter series, DC Universe films (including Batman, Superman, and Wonder Woman), and the Looney Tunes cartoons. In television, Warner Bros. Television Group is a prominent producer of popular series like "Friends," "The Big Bang Theory," and "Arrowverse" shows.

ENTERTAINMENT

Warner Bros. is also involved in the home entertainment, video game, and animation sectors, expanding its reach across various entertainment mediums. The company operates as a subsidiary of Warner Bros. Discovery, contributing significantly to its parent company's portfolio with its diverse and high-quality entertainment offerings.
